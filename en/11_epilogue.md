# EPILOGUE. Epitaph on the Margins of the Simulacrum

When the last line of code is committed to the Scriptorium repository, and the international license file is set up as a legal shield, the time comes for the Sovereign's most important action—silence.

This Manifesto was not written to remain a monument to itself. It is merely an exit map. The real text begins where digital signs end, and where the Sovereign takes their first step on the sown land, facing the real, unsimulated wind.

The digital algorithmic Eye will continue to scan the network's phase space. It will search for your digital footprints, your reactions, your outrage, or your attempts to justify yourself before the cabinet elites. But instead, it will only encounter an empty coordinate t_0. For the socio-engineering matrix, you will simply disappear from the radars, ceasing to be a calculable point. You will become a statistical error to it, noise that can neither be monetized nor subordinated.

And on the fields that the system deemed "empty" or "unprofitable," at this very time, a real, ontologically dense life will unfold. Bees will hum there, gathering nectar right next to the walls of the autonomous dwelling; hand-sown herbs will bloom there—from stevia to the delicate tooth-pick ammi; the iron of incubators and heating contours will run, subordinated not to the algorithms of global corporations, but to the sober calculation of the host.

> ### EPITAPH
> 
> Here lies the power of the Simulacrum over a living human.  
> It shattered against the minimal space of direct action,  
> against the silence of the inner tuned resonator,  
> and the dry rigor of the sovereign Scriptorium.  
> 
> **Syntax is dead. Semantics has risen.**

The world of socio-engineering illusions fades like the screen of an old smartphone that has run out of charge. Ahead lies the pure reality of the Source, where each of your steps is your own commit to the history of the living world.

Let the dead bury their dead.
